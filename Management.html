TOMAL BANERJEE

Technical manager of Etronics

Mr. Tomal is a result oriented professional with 10 years’ experience in Project management activities in Electronics, IT and Networking industries. A keen implementer with sufficient experience of handling commissioning activities involving resource planning, process enhancement, team building and coordination with internal and external departments. His main areas of expertise include Management Consulting, Business Process Improvement, Team Leadership, Strategic Planning, Budget setting and performance review, Business Strategy, Business Analysis, IT Strategy, Testing and Validation of Electronic Systems, Strong knowledge on network hardware including servers, routers and other computer devices, network security protocol, anti-virus and firewall configuration, hardware related training activities and Organizational Structuring. Prior to his association with Etronics Engineers LLP, he engaged in installing, commissioning, maintaining of railway safe devices and supervise the network in North-east Frontier railway. He is also engaged with network based organization which provides internet service (ISP) to different organizations and home clients in West Bengal. He obtained his Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering under West Bengal University of Technology, India and Executive Master Program in Business Administration in Telecom Management, IIBM, India.
